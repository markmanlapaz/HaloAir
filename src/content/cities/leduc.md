## City of Leduc permits

The City of Leduc handles permits independently — Planning and Development is the right contact. Most residential furnace and AC installs need a Building Permit; gas work needs a Gas Permit. Typical turnaround is 1–3 business days.

We handle the pull-file-inspect cycle end-to-end. The permit line item lives in your written quote, transparent and itemized.
