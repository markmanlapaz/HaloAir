## Town of Beaumont permits

Beaumont permits are independent of Edmonton's — Planning and Development is the right contact at the Town office. Most residential HVAC and gas work needs a Building Permit + Gas Permit. Turnaround tends to be quick — 1–3 business days for routine residential work.

As with every Halo Air install: permits are part of the quote, we file them, we coordinate inspections, you get the green tag.
