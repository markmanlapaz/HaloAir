## Spruce Grove permits

The City of Spruce Grove Planning and Building Services handles permits independently of Edmonton. Most residential HVAC installs need a Building Permit; gas appliance work needs a Gas Permit. Turnaround is fast — usually 1–3 business days.

We handle every step: pulling the permit, filing it, scheduling the inspection, and delivering the green tag at the end of the install.
