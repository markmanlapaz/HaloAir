SECTION 1
What We Are Building
We are building you two things that work together seamlessly — a professional website for your HVAC business, and a smart AI dispatcher that handles your booking and scheduling automatically.
Your Website
A fast, modern, mobile-friendly website that ranks well on Google and makes it easy for customers to find you, understand your services, and get in touch. It will be built to perform — meaning real customers searching for HVAC services in your area will find you.
Your website will include:
Home page — professional first impression, clear call to action
Services pages — individual pages for AC repair, furnace installation, maintenance, and more
About page — who you are, your experience, certifications
Contact &amp; booking form — easy for customers to request a service call
Service area pages — targeting the specific cities and regions you cover
Your AI Smart Dispatcher
This is the part that sets your business apart. When a customer fills out a booking request on your website, an AI system takes over and handles the scheduling for you — automatically.
Here is exactly what happens:
Customer submits a service request on your website
The AI reads the request and checks your Google Calendar for availability
The AI books the appointment into your calendar
The customer receives an automatic confirmation email
You see the appointment appear in your Google Calendar — nothing else to do
You stay in control. Before the AI officially confirms any booking, you receive a notification and can approve or reschedule with one tap. Think of it as a 24/7 receptionist that never sleeps and never misses a call.
